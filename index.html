About Last updated: 2019-06-01

I am a PhD student at IIIT Allahabad under supervision Dr. Ratan K Saha. I am working at Biomedical Imaging Lab (BMIL) on Photoacoustics. I completed my Bachelors from DDMC, WBSU,Kolkata. My PhD research focuses on computational simulation on single and multiple cell aggregate comparatively dense and sparse ambient media understanding. I have a deep passion for programming, and I explore the CoA and acoustics research. I finished my M.Sc with "Swami Vivekananda Merit-cum-Means Scholarship (Govt. of West Bengal)" in computational physics from University of Gour Banga. During my M.Sc i did a project under Dr. Subrata Sarkar. The project title is "Numerical simulation based Si tandem solar cell". I am currently researching with UGC Fellowship through JOINT CSIR - UGC NET EXAMINATION JUNE 2020.
